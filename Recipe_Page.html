Simple Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oliva oil
  • Tomato Sauce

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large vessel.
  2. Add salt and pasta to the boiling water.
  3. Cook pasta for 8-10 minutes.
  4. Drain the water.
  5. Add tomato sauce and olive oil,then mix well
